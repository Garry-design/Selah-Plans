The Name

"Selah"

The Hebrew word selah appears 74 times in the Psalms. Its precise meaning is debated — but most scholars understand it as a pause, a breath, a moment to stop and let what was just said sink in.

In an age of endless noise and constant motion, Selah Plans invites you to stop. To sit with Scripture not as a productivity task, but as a meeting with the living God. To pause long enough to be met by the One who knows every season of your heart.
