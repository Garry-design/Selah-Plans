The Name

Selah — a sacred pause

The Hebrew word selah appears 74 times in the Psalms. Its precise meaning is debated — but most scholars understand it as a pause, a breath, a moment to stop and let what was just said sink in.

In an age of endless noise and constant motion, Selah Plans invites you to stop. To sit with Scripture not as a productivity task, but as a meeting with the living God.
