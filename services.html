How It Works

Start in under a minute

No account, no setup, no onboarding. Just choose a mood and begin.

Begin Now →
1

Choose your emotional season

Select from 15 moods — or use the daily check-in if you're not sure where to start.

2

Select or generate a plan

Pick a built-in plan, or describe your situation to the AI generator for something fully personalised.

3

Open the day's passage

Each day gives you a Scripture reference (with a link to Bible Gateway), a reflection question, and a prayer.

4

Reflect, pray, take notes

Sit with the reflection. Pray the written prayer or use your own. Add notes in the text field — they're saved locally.

5

Mark complete and return tomorrow

Your progress is tracked automatically. Return tomorrow and the next day is ready and waiting.
