What Selah Plans Offers

Everything you need for a meaningful reading practice

Built-in plans, AI generation, flexible tools, and a focused reading experience — all in one place.

📖

Built-in Mood Plans

Eight hand-crafted plans across seven emotional seasons — anxiety, grief, doubt, fear, joy, gratitude, and loneliness. Each plan features carefully chosen Scripture passages, reflection prompts, and prayers written specifically for that season.

Core Feature
✨

AI Custom Generator

Describe your emotional season in your own words. Set the duration (3 to 30 days), choose a reading style (devotional, deep study, or narrative), and select thematic emphases. The AI generates a structured, personalised plan with Scripture, reflections, and prayers.

AI-Powered
✏️

Editable Day Entries

After generating a custom plan, every day entry is editable. Change the Scripture reference, rewrite the reflection prompt, or update the prayer. Individual days can also be regenerated with a single click if the content doesn't fit your season.

Flexible
🔖

Save and Load Plans

Plans are saved locally to your browser so your progress persists. Pick up exactly where you left off, across built-in and custom plans. Each saved plan tracks your completed days and overall progress independently.

Persistent
📤

Export and Import JSON

Export any saved plan as a JSON file to back it up, share it, or move it between devices. Import previously exported plans back into your library. Your plans belong to you — you can take them with you anywhere.

Portable
🎲

Daily Tools

A random verse generator for those days when you want to open Scripture and be surprised. A daily check-in prompt to name how you're feeling and orient your reading. Small tools for the rhythm of a daily practice.

Daily Practice
📋

Dedicated Plan View

A distraction-free reading page designed for focused daily engagement. Navigate between days, mark days as complete, track your progress bar, and read your reflection and prayer without the surrounding interface getting in the way.

Focus Mode
🔍

Mood Search and Filters

Search and filter the built-in plan library by mood, tag, or duration. Quickly find plans that match your season without scrolling through everything. Plans are tagged with multiple thematic keywords for more precise filtering.

Organised
👤

Simple Account System

A lightweight local account system lets you save your progress under a name without requiring any external login or data sharing. All data stays in your browser. No subscriptions, no tracking, no ads.

Private
Reading Experience

Built for depth, not speed

The Plan View page removes every distraction and puts just the day's content in front of you. No sidebar menus, no stats panels, no UI clutter — just the Scripture reference, the reflection question, and the prayer.

Navigate between days with simple forward and back controls. Mark each day complete when you're ready. Watch your progress bar grow. Return anytime — your progress is always saved.
