Our Story

A place to pause
and meet God

Selah Plans was built on a simple conviction: the Bible has something to say to every human emotion — anxiety, grief, shame, joy, anger, and everything in between. The question is finding the right door in.

Most Bible reading plans assume you arrive ready to learn. Selah Plans assumes you arrive exactly as you are — tired, afraid, grateful, angry, broken, or bursting. And from there, it helps you meet the living God.

The Name

What is Selah?

The Hebrew word selah appears 74 times in the Psalms. Its precise meaning is debated — but most scholars understand it as a pause. A breath. A moment for the words just spoken to settle into the soul.

In an age of relentless pace and perpetual noise, we believe this is exactly what Scripture reading needs — not speed, not completion, not efficiency. Just stopping. Sitting. Listening.

What We Believe

Built on a few convictions

📍
Meet people where they are
The Psalms don't pretend. Jesus met people in the middle of their storms, not after. So do we. Every plan starts with your actual emotional reality — not where you think you should be.
🤲
Scripture does the work
We curate the door. God's Word does the healing, comforting, convicting, and transforming. We never try to replace the text — we try to help you slow down enough to actually receive it.
⏳
Unhurried is faithful
Reading the Bible through in a year has its place. But sometimes faithfulness looks like sitting with one passage for a week. Selah Plans honours slowness as a spiritual discipline.
💬
Honest prayer matters
The prayers in each plan are written to model honest, specific, unsentimental prayer — because that's the kind God answers. Not polished performance, but real conversation.
🎁
Free always
Scripture should not be behind a paywall. Selah Plans is free because access to God's Word should never depend on whether you can afford a subscription.
🏠
A companion, not a replacement
Selah Plans is a companion to your local church, your pastor, your community. Not a substitute. We never want to become what you avoid the hard work of real Christian community to use instead.
Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need an account?+
No account required to browse or use plans. If you enter a name in the plans page, your progress is saved locally in your browser. No server, no password, no email — just your device.
Is it really free?+
Yes. Completely free. No subscription, no premium tier, no ads. The AI generator makes calls directly to the Anthropic API — which does use API credits — but the built-in plans are entirely free to use.
Which Bible translation do you use?+
We don't include full text — we provide Scripture references, which you can read in your preferred translation. Each plan links directly to Bible Gateway so you can read in NIV, ESV, NLT, or whichever version you prefer.
Can I export my generated plans?+
Yes — any plan (built-in or generated) can be exported as a JSON file and imported back later, even on a different device. This is a practical way to back up progress or share a plan with someone.
How do I use Selah Plans alongside my church?+
Selah Plans works best as a personal devotional tool — something you use in your own quiet time, alongside Sunday services, community groups, and pastoral care. It's not designed to replace any of those things, but to supplement your private engagement with Scripture.
I'm going through something serious. Is this enough?+
Selah Plans is a starting point, not a substitute for pastoral care, counselling, or professional support. If you're in crisis — emotionally, spiritually, or practically — please reach out to your pastor, a trusted friend, or a mental health professional. This app is a companion on the journey, not a destination.
