Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is Selah Plans free to use? ▾
Yes — entirely. There are no subscriptions, no paywalls, and no accounts required. The built-in plans and all core features are free. The AI custom generator uses the Anthropic API and is available to use freely in this version.
Do I need to create an account? ▾
No account is required. You can set a name for a personalised experience, but it's entirely optional and stored only in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. All your plan data and progress is saved locally.
Where is my data stored? ▾
Everything is stored in your browser's local storage. This means your plans and progress are private to your device and never sent to us. If you clear your browser data, your plans will be lost — that's why we recommend exporting important plans as JSON to back them up.
Which Bible translation does Selah Plans use? ▾
Selah Plans provides Scripture references rather than full text, encouraging you to read in your preferred translation. The reflection prompts and content are written in a translation-neutral way, drawing from the NIV but readily applicable across translations.
Can I suggest a new mood category or plan? ▾
Absolutely — we'd love to hear your suggestions. Use the contact form above and select "Feedback or feature request." We're genuinely building this with our community in mind.
Can I use Selah Plans in my church or small group? ▾
Yes! You're welcome to use Selah Plans in your church, small group, or faith community. Plans can be exported as JSON and shared with others. If you'd like to discuss a more structured partnership, please reach out via email.
